The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sprider-Man

The story follows Peter as he learns to balance his new powers and responsibilities while battling his first major villain, the Green Goblin, portrayed by Willem Dafoe.

Avengers: Infinity War

The Avengers team up with the Guardians of the Galaxy to stop Thanos from collecting the Infinity Stones and wiping out half of all life.

Titanic (1997)

A timeless romance and disaster film about the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic.
